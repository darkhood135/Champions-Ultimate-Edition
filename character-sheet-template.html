Spider-Man

Peter Parker

Striker

Gifted with superhuman agility, reflexes, and a brilliant mind, Peter Parker balances the weight of responsibility with the power to protect. As Spider-Man, he weaves through danger with web-slinging grace, using his intellect and speed to outmaneuver stronger foes. On the battlefield, he's a scrappy, reactive striker — landing precision hits, dodging through openings, and punishing enemies who overcommit. When Spider-Man's back is against the wall, that's when he's most dangerous.

"With great power, comes great responsibility."

Changelog

Balance changes since this Champion's release. Newest first.

v1.2.3 May 19, 2026
  • Buff ATK raised from 380 → 390.
  • Buff Rapid Web Onslaught — cooldown reduced from 2 turns to 1 turn.

Spider-Man was underperforming in mid-tier PvP. Small tune-up to keep his web-slinging pressure relevant against the current Arena meta without pushing him into broken territory.

v1.2.0 April 15, 2026
  • Nerf Spider Sense Surge — counter damage reduced from 120% ATK to 100% ATK.
  • Rework Web Bind — infliction chance lowered 80% → 70%, range increased 5 → 6.

The counter loop was dominating low-level Arena where most teams couldn't punch through it. Slight range buff on Web Bind to preserve his control identity now that the counters hit less hard.

v1.1.0 March 4, 2026
  • Buff New synergy added with Spider-Gwen: "Across the Spider-Verse".

Spider-Gwen released and the thematic pairing was an obvious add. Both Champions accumulate Web Fluid faster and share stacks.

v1.0.0 January 1, 2026
  • Release Spider-Man enters the roster.
