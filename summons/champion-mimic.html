Champion Mimic

Prismatic Echo

Adaptive

When the Summoner fixes on a combatant and reaches, the walls between worlds thin, and something steps through wearing a face already present on the battlefield. The Champion Mimic is no illusion and no puppet, but a living echo — the very same person, plucked out of a reality that turned out differently, carrying the scars of a life this world never lived. Called to an ally's side, it fights as a second self, doubling a hero's strength with someone who learned all the same lessons by other roads. Called against a foe, it is something far crueler: a mirror given hands, forcing an enemy to trade blows with living proof of who they might have been had they only chosen otherwise. And when the fighting is done and the Mimic dissolves back toward whatever world it was torn from, there is always a moment — brief, and seldom spoken of afterward — where it turns and looks at the Summoner as though it recognizes something no one else in any reality can see.

Passive Abilities
Adaptive Form Passive 1
  • Channels the multiversal energy of The Summoner to transform into the selected enemy or ally Champion (chosen by The Summoner).
  • Transforming into a Champion utilizes their start-of-battle state (cooldowns, status effects, etc.).
  • The transformation matches the exact loadout of the copied Champion, including their ULTIMATUM — which begins at 0% ULT and charges through that Champion's own generation conditions.
  • The transformation does not gain buffs of any kind due to links.
  • The transformation matches all stats and vitals as the copied Champion but retains a MAX HP of 3,000.
  • Persists until defeated (no duration limit).
Limits of Mimicry Passive 2
  • Cannot receive healing from any source, including its own abilities.
  • Cannot receive buffs from allies (self excluded).
  • Defeated instantly if The Summoner is defeated.
Changelog

Balance changes since this Summon's release. Newest first.

v1.0.1 July 11, 2026
  • Rework Role changed from Booster to Adaptive — a new Summon-only role for constructs with no fixed combat identity. Added to the Summons role filter.
  • Rework Adaptive Form — clarified that the transformation inherits the copied Champion's ULTIMATUM, beginning at 0% ULT and charging through that Champion's own generation conditions. "Matches all stats" corrected to "all stats and vitals." Added: persists until defeated (no duration limit).
  • Rework Limits of Mimicry — healing restriction clarified to "cannot receive healing from any source, including its own abilities."

Clarification pass with no intended power change. The Champion Mimic released with several load-bearing rules undefined, and each one materially changes how the summon plays. ULTIMATUM inheritance is the largest: copying a Champion's full loadout implies access to their ultimate, and stating the 0% starting charge makes clear it is a rare payoff rather than an immediate one. The stats/vitals wording mattered because the Catechism treats Vitals (MAX EN, MAX STA, REGEN) as separate from Stats, and the Mimic needs energy to use a copied kit at all. The healing clarification is the most consequential in practice — many potential copy targets carry self-healing in their own kits, and the previous wording left it ambiguous whether those bypassed the restriction; they do not, keeping the flat 3,000 HP pool a genuine burst-down window. The role change replaces a template leftover that described nothing in the summon's kit.
