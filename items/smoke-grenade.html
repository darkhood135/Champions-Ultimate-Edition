Smoke Grenade

Throwable

A compressed canister of dense particulate haze. When thrown, it bursts into a thick cloud that obscures sight lines and disrupts enemy aim — cheap, reliable, and the favorite tool of anyone who needs to disappear in a hurry. Especially common in stealth-leaning Champions' kits and on urban battlefields where cover is already at a premium.

Effect

Item Ability
Deploy Smoke Throwable
Type: Throwable · Target: One Panel + AoE (3 panels) · Range: 6
  • Generates a smoke cloud in a 3-panel radius around the targeted panel.
  • All Champions inside the cloud (allies and enemies) are inflicted with "Disorient" for 2 turns.
  • The cloud blocks line of sight, preventing ranged abilities from passing through it for 2 turns.
  • Consumed on use.
