Road to Redemption

The Mindset link for Champions actively trying to make up for what they used to be. Whether they were villains, killers, or just on the wrong side of a war they didn't fully understand, Road to Redemption Champions carry their pasts with them as motivation rather than baggage. They tend to fight harder than they need to, hold themselves to standards they didn't grow up with, and find their best moments when their old skills get put to use for new reasons.
