Batgirl

Cassandra Cain

Striker

Cassandra Cain is the Batgirl who was raised as a weapon and trained to read combat the way most people read words. Denied speech as a child by her father David Cain, she learned to interpret the world entirely through movement — the twitch before a swing, the breath before a strike, the intent in a stranger's stance. She joined the Bat-Family looking for atonement and stayed because she found the closest thing she'd ever had to a home. In combat she's a counter-Striker who turns every fight into a chess match she's already played: Tracking Batarangs mark her targets, What Comes Next punishes anyone reckless enough to swing first, and Image of Justice scales harder the longer her enemies make her wait.

"My name is Cassandra Cain...and I am no oracle. I do not tell the future. I know the past. I see the now. Faces. Movements. Patterns. Nerves. Intentions. Mistakes. My sight is my prophecy, and my fists are my medium. I simply read the cards laid out before me...and I play my hand. I am Batgirl. I write the fortunes of my enemies. No matter the hand I was dealt. I make my future. And I always will."
