The Pirate

Still Life Entity

Striker

The Pirate is a Still Life — a flawed dimensional copy of Clark, a furniture store owner whose mascot costume the Backrooms decided to replicate in eight feet of distorted, barely-proportioned flesh. Peg leg made of chair wood. Arms that don't fit the body. Face permanently set to a frown. He bleeds when hurt, which shouldn't be possible for something like him — it hasn't stopped him. He hunts by proximity, moving toward the nearest warm body with the unhurried certainty of something that doesn't need to hurry, and he is considerably stronger than he has any right to be.

"She says we don't have to change!"
