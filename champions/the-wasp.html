The Wasp

Janet Van Dyne

Healer

A brilliant heiress who gave Earth's Mightiest Heroes their very name, Janet van Dyne fights as the Wasp — the winged, stinging heart and soul of the Avengers.

Everyone underestimated Janet van Dyne, and one by one she proved every last one of them wrong. A glamorous heiress who first took up the fight to avenge her murdered father, she was written off in her early days as the team's socialite — all wit and fashion and flightiness — a hero no one thought to take seriously. But beneath the glamour was a spine of pure steel, and across the decades she grew into something none of them saw coming: not just a founding Avenger, but one of the finest leaders the team ever had, the chairwoman who held the line and turned back the Masters of Evil when the Avengers were at their lowest. She endured a marriage that nearly broke her and walked out the other side wholly her own woman, defined by no one but herself. Through it all she has remained the emotional core of Earth's Mightiest Heroes — the warmth that binds a room of clashing egos into a family, forever reinventing herself into more than anyone expected her to be.

That reinvention is built on the science that changed her life. Infused with Pym particles, Janet can shrink to the size of an insect or tower as a giant in an instant, and at her smallest a pair of translucent wings unfurls to carry her through the air with darting, impossible speed. She strikes with the "wasp's sting" — bursts of bio-electric energy fired from her hands that punch far above her size — and fights with an aerial grace that makes her a maddening, near-untouchable target. But the particles were never what made her formidable. It's that a woman the world dismissed as ornamental became its fiercest protector, and taught a team of titans that the smallest of them could be its greatest heart.

"A hundred years from now, when we're all dead and buried, there will be only two things that we're remembered for... what we did. And what we looked like. Our uniforms are as much a part of our legacy as anything we'll ever do."

Changelog

Balance changes since this Champion's release. Newest first.

v1.0.1 May 27, 2026
  • Nerf Expert of Pym Particles and Bioenergy (Core Passive) — Sting's per-heal bonus reduced from 100% → 70% MAG; maximum bonus reduced from +500% → +350% MAG. Maximum Sting damage is now 650% MAG (down from 800%).
  • Nerf Expert of Pym Particles and Bioenergy (Core Passive) — EN refund per heal or Overhealth grant reduced from 10 → 8 EN.
  • Rework Stinging Surge — Target now also loses 1 STA in addition to the existing 5 EN penalty, spreading the cost across two resources to better reflect the "sting" of the application.

Sting's damage ceiling at 800% MAG placed a Healer above most Strikers' peak single-target output when fully charged, even accounting for the opportunity cost of banking heals. The reduced cap keeps the scaling mechanic intact and rewarding while landing at a more appropriate ceiling for the role. The EN refund reduction is a measured trim for consistency with the adjusted Sting economy. The Stinging Surge STA penalty better represents the bioelectric cost of the application without making it prohibitively expensive for the recipient.
