Wolverine

James "Logan" Howlett

Striker

A century-old mutant with unbreakable claws, a stolen past, and a healing factor that will not let him die, Logan fights alongside the X-Men as Wolverine — the best there is at what he does.

Logan has lived long enough to bury nearly everyone he ever loved, and he keeps letting himself love people anyway. Born James Howlett more than a hundred years ago, he has worn a dozen names across a dozen wars, and much of what he remembers of it is a lie — the Weapon X program that flooded his skeleton with adamantium also carved up his mind, leaving a man who cannot fully trust his own past. What that torture left behind was an animal, and the truest thing about him is the daily, grinding war he wages to keep it caged. He is a killer who works to be something better than a killer, holding to a hard-earned code of honor and a rigid line about who deserves his claws, knowing all the while how easily the berserker beneath takes the wheel. For all his snarling and his loner's posture, he is drawn again and again into the role he'd never claim out loud — the gruff protector, the mentor who takes in frightened young mutants and teaches them to survive a world that hates them. He carries more grief and more red in his ledger than any man should, and he keeps standing between that world and the people in it who can't stand for themselves.

The body that carries him through it is a weapon in every sense. A mutant healing factor knits flesh, bone, and organ back together from wounds that would kill anything else, staving off disease and poison and slowing his aging to a crawl — pairing with senses so keen he can track a man by scent across miles. His entire skeleton is laced with indestructible adamantium, and from each forearm spring three claws of the same metal, able to shear through nearly any substance in existence. Add a century of combat mastered in every corner of the world and there are few living things that can stand against him. But the claws were never the hardest part of being Wolverine. That's the man holding them back — choosing, every single day, what to be.

"I'm Wolverine. I'm the best there is at what I do, but what I do best isn't very nice."

The Weight of War w/ Captain America

Logan has followed a lot of men into a lot of wars, and Steve Rogers is the only one he's never once questioned. They're of the same vintage — two soldiers who were there for the worst of the last century — and that shared weight says more between them than words ever have. Logan knows full well that Cap disapproves of how he gets things done, and respects him the more for it; when Captain America gives an order, the old animal falls in without a word of argument.

Claws and Conscience w/ Spider-Man

Peter Parker is everything Logan stopped being long before the kid was ever born — clean hands, an unbroken code, and a mouth that jokes its way through the pain instead of snarling at it. Logan quietly counts him among the very best of them, and long ago appointed himself the boy's protector without once admitting it aloud. They agree on almost nothing, and there are damn few people the old man would rather have at his back.

Bad Blood w/ The Punisher

Logan has no use for Frank Castle. He's spent a century learning exactly where the lines are and paying dearly for every time he crossed one, and in Frank he sees a man who erased them altogether and calls the wreckage a war. They've put each other through hell more than once, and neither is inclined to let it go; when the two wind up on the same side of a fight, it's a temporary accident, and both are counting the minutes.

Changelog

Balance changes since this Champion's release. Newest first.

v1.0.2 July 11, 2026
  • Nerf Wild Fury (Passive — Reserve) — ATK stacking is now capped at 10 stacks (+50% ATK), previously unlimited.

Wild Fury was the only uncapped scaling value in Wolverine's kit. Its intended limiter — losing all stacks on switching targets or using a multi-target attack — has no bite in extended single-target content, where there is never a reason to switch off the target; across a long boss encounter in Crisis on Infinite Earths or the Dark Dimension the buff could climb past +200% ATK and turn Berserker Barrage into a runaway. The 10-stack ceiling preserves the ability's identity — lock onto one enemy and escalate, reaching maximum after roughly eight to ten committed abilities — while bringing it in line with the bounded stacking buffs used elsewhere in the roster (Miles's Learning Responsibility and Robin's ally buffs). Only the focused single-target build is affected.

v1.0.1 May 30, 2026
  • Nerf MAG — reduced from 200 to 0. Logan has no MAG-scaling abilities.
  • Rework SPD / Weapon X — base SPD reduced from 116 to 100; Weapon X gains new effect: +2% SPD per exact 10% of HP remaining (of MAX HP). SPD now scales dynamically between 100 (at or below 10% HP) and 120 (at 100% HP).
  • Rework Weapon X — resurrection now restores 30% HP; limited to once per battle. ULT generation capped at once per ability (was uncapped per damage instance).
  • Rework Adamantium Skeleton — Last Stand threshold clarified as 90% of MAX HP; limited to once per battle.
  • Rework Tornado Claw — range reduced from 5 to 3 panels; cooldown increased from 2 to 3 turns; base damage increased from 200% to 350% ATK. Extension now reaches 5 panels (was 7) on a whiff.
  • Nerf Lethal Pounce — range reduced from 7 to 5 panels; airborne range penalty adjusted from −5 to −3 (effective anti-air range unchanged at 2).
  • Rework Brutal Pierce — base damage reduced from 550% to 400% ATK; DEF-ignore removed; now deals a guaranteed critical hit; Bleed infliction chance increased from 40% to 70%.
  • Rework Rapid Healing — now usable at any point during Logan's turn; ends Wolverine's turn after usage. Retains ability to be used while unable to act.
  • Rework Primal Rage — Indestructible duration reduced from 2 turns to 1 turn; added +10% ATK and SPD for 1 turn.
  • Rework Wild Fury — clarified to stack once per ability use (not per damage instance within a multi-hit ability).
  • Rework Sharpened Senses — costume effect changed from +5% EVA, DEF and RES to +5% EVA and SPD.
  • Rework Scars and Bruises — costume effect changed from +12% MAG, −6% ATK and SPD to +70% DEF and RES, −14% ATK, −3% SPD.

Wolverine's base SPD has been redistributed into a dynamic HP-scaling mechanic, making his turn priority a live measurement of his combat state — at full health he's the fastest Champion in the roster, but sustained damage progressively costs him action priority. The ULT cap and range reductions address the previous version's excessive ULT charge rate and gap-closing reach; Logan now has to earn engagement range with STA investment before the pounce-barrage chain is accessible. Rapid Healing uses "ends turn" as its sole constraint — freely usable at any point but consuming the remainder of the turn, cleanly preserving the CC-break fantasy without additional conditions. Brutal Pierce gains a guaranteed critical hit, making it the reliable precision finisher in the Wild Fury stack build while Berserker Barrage remains the volume damage standard. Primal Rage becomes an offensive last-stand push rather than a passive 2-turn survival island, consistent with Logan's feral identity.
