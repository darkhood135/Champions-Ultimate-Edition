Spider-Man

Peter Parker

Striker

Spider-Man — Peter Parker to the people who know him — is Queens' favorite wallcrawler and Marvel's most enduring underdog. A radioactive spider bite gave him superhuman strength, reflexes, and a precognitive danger sense; his own engineering brilliance gave him the web-shooters. He wins fights he shouldn't through pure motion: clinging to walls, swinging between rooftops, dodging blows he saw a half-second early. Perpetually broke, perpetually outclassed on paper, and never standing still long enough to be hit twice. He cracks jokes through a bloody nose, lifts impossible weights for people who'll never thank him, and shows up — exhausted, late, alone — every single time. With great power comes great responsibility, and nobody's carried that burden longer.

"With great power, comes great responsibility."

Changelog

Balance changes since this Champion's release. Newest first.

v1.0.1 May 19, 2026
  • Nerf Spider Strength — bonus damage per 10 EN remaining reduced from 100% ATK → 50% ATK.
  • Rework Web Swing — can now target a panel in addition to an obstacle. Panel-targeting relocates Spider-Man 5 panels in the direction of the targeted panel.

At full EN, Spider Strength was capable of dealing close to 1,800% ATK on a single target — disproportionately powerful versus comparable ULTIMATUMs. The new scaling caps the burst around 1,300% ATK at full EN, keeping the empty-the-tank fantasy without one-shotting tanks. Web Swing was a clean mobility tool but completely dead on obstacle-free stages; the panel-direction option ensures it has utility on any battlefield.
