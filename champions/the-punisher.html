The Punisher

Francis Castle

Striker

Frank Castle buried his family and declared war on crime — not metaphorically. Two tours of combat gave him the tools; Central Park took away every reason to hold back. The Punisher doesn't deter criminals or arrest them. He ends them, methodically, because someone has to, and Frank decided a long time ago that someone would be him.

"These men believe they are agents of death. The terrorists think that they bring war. But they don't know how long I've spent in the company of death and war. They don't know... death and war are my allies."

Changelog

Balance changes since this Champion's release. Newest first.

v1.1.0 May 30, 2026
  • Buff MAX STA — increased from 7 to 8.
  • Rework Deliverance — base damage increased from 500% to 600% MAG; falloff floor raised to a minimum of 120% MAG; CRIT RATE bonus increased from +10% to +20%; added STA restore on use (up to 7 STA at range 1, decreasing by 1 per additional panel of distance, minimum 1).
  • Buff Knife Tactics — Slashing Blade Bleed extension increased from +1 to +2 turns; new effect: +10% ATK and MAG when targeting a Bleed-afflicted enemy.
  • Buff Tactical Reload — now also restores 2 STA on use.
  • Buff Quick Thinking of a Soldier — obstacle damage bonus increased from 30% to 50%; new effect: +50% DEF and RES against throwable items; proximity STA restore increased from 1 to 2 per trigger; proximity now also restores 5 EN per trigger.
  • Buff Unrequited Respect (synergy) — Anger duration granted to Captain America on enemy defeat increased from 1 to 2 turns.

Frank's previous version suffered from extreme STA constraints and poor cohesion between his melee opener and ranged kit. Deliverance now fully supports the intended Slashing Blade combo — point-blank STA restore lets Frank charge in, knife, blast, and reposition in a single engagement. Knife Tactics' Bleed interaction makes the knife opener meaningful for his MAG output over the following turns, resolving the melee-in-a-ranged-kit tension. Quick Thinking's EN and STA components make aggressive close-range play a viable sustained strategy. The MAX STA increase and Tactical Reload's STA addition collectively improve his movement budget without compromising the long-range Sniper archetype.
