Cloak

Tyrone Johnson

Breaker

Tyrone Johnson never intended to be a superhero. He and Tandy were teenagers on the run from bad luck and worse circumstances when a mob chemist decided they'd make good test subjects — and the Darkforce claimed Tyrone before the drug could kill him. Now he carries a literal dimension of consuming void in his chest, and the only thing that holds the hunger at bay is the light Tandy puts into him. In battle, Cloak is a Darkness engine: flooding the enemy line with his signature infliction, stealing their buffs for himself, locking down their supports through Chaining Essence, and sustaining off every kill his void earns — a disruption machine that hits harder the more the Darkness spreads.

"We've got to make a pact Tandy...a vow if we're gonna survive this. I swear I will always be there for you, Do you hear me?"

Changelog

Balance changes since this Champion's release. Newest first.

v1.1.0 June 29, 2026
  • Rework Role changed from Booster to Breaker. Cloak's kit is refocused around the "Darkness" status as a disruption-and-damage engine — trading broad team buffs for enemy disruption, damage amplification and self-sustain.
  • Nerf MAX HP reduced 7,200 → 6,400. RES reduced 130 → 110. EVA reduced 20% → 10%.
  • Rework Master of the Darkforce (Core): no longer grants allies MAG or infliction chance, and no longer grants EVA against ranged attacks. Now grants +8% MAG when attacking a "Darkness"-inflicted enemy, and restores 8% HP, 10 EN and 2 STA whenever a "Darkness"-inflicted enemy is defeated. ULT gain on attack/steal retained.
  • Nerf Cover Rescue: rescued allies no longer gain EN. Cooldown increased 3 → 4 turns. Can now only be used as the first action of the turn.
  • Buff Cover Rescue: now inflicts "Darkness" for 2 turns on each enemy whose attack is intercepted.
  • Rework Terror Cape moved into the main lineup (swapped with Dark Teleportation), supporting the new Darkness engine. Target line corrected to "All Enemies in 3-Wide Line".
  • Nerf Dark Teleportation moved to reserve; targeted ally no longer gains EN.
  • Rework Embrace the Darkness: no longer grants allies ATK and MAG. Energy reduced 60 → 40, cooldown reduced 5 → 3 turns, range increased 3 → 4 panels. Still applies "Dark Vessel" to allies in range.
  • Nerf Darkforce Drain: stolen buffs are now always applied to self instead of the nearest ally. Damage unchanged.
  • Rework Chaining Essence: "Constraint" now lands guaranteed only if the target is already inflicted with "Darkness" (otherwise 30% chance) and the ability no longer self-inflicts "Darkness". Allies no longer restore EN. Now interrupts and inflicts "Constraint" (1 turn) on any enemy that targets the constrained enemy with a Support ability (does not propagate).
  • Nerf Binding Pact: no longer grants ATK; ally stat buff reduced 10% → 7% (now MAG and HEAL).
  • Rework Soulmates: no longer grants EVA against ranged attacks while Dagger is present. Now grants +7% MAG while Dagger is present.
  • Buff Exhausting Presence: STA steal increased 2 → 3.
  • Nerf Creeping Death: MAG bonus reduced 20% → 15%. "Stealth" gain from teleporting abilities now limited to once per 2 turns.
  • Rework Darkness Unleashed moved to the primary ULTIMATUM slot. No longer reduces DEF or RES; instead, all enemies within the domain take 15% more damage (after defenses, stacking with "Darkness"). −10% EVA retained.
  • Rework Light in the Darkness moved to the reserve ULTIMATUM slot (effect unchanged).
At v1.0.0 Cloak was a do-everything Booster — elite stacked team buffs, a Plunderer's buff theft and resource drain, a Breaker's debuff domain and crowd control, plus caster-tier MAG and a backline-defying HP/EVA pool. The breadth, not any single number, made him overtuned. This pass commits him to a single identity: a Breaker who spreads "Darkness," amplifies the damage his team deals through it, locks down enemy supports, and sustains himself off "Darkness" kills. Team-wide stat buffing is largely removed, survivability is pulled back to a mid-line value, and reliable "Darkness" application is built into his main lineup (Cover Rescue, Terror Cape, Embrace) so the engine stays online.
