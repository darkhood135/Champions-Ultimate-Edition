Brooklyn Rooftops

Brooklyn, New York — Street Level

Marvel Universe

A stretch of pre-war tenement rooftops over the Brooklyn waterfront. Air conditioner housings, water towers and skylights break up sightlines; the gaps between buildings demand traversal abilities or careful timing. The fight is fast, vertical and close enough to the street that civilians become a real consideration — but the rooftops themselves are abandoned, claimed only by pigeons and whoever was already running across them when the fight started. The terrain rewards Champions who can move between elevations without being forced into the alleys below.

"Eight million stories on these rooftops. Tonight, ours."

Obstacles & Terrain

Each obstacle's color matches its map panel and key swatch
Destructible Cover
AC
AC Housing Destructible Cover
Footprint: 1 panel · Blocks: Line of Sight, Projectiles · HP: 600 · Cover Value: 40% DEF
  • Allies adjacent to this obstacle gain +40% DEF against ranged attacks originating from the opposite side.
  • Destroying this obstacle drops a debris field on its tile for 2 turns, which counts as difficult terrain (costs 2 STA to move into).
  • Can be picked up and thrown by Champions with Strength: 5+ as a 250% ATK ranged attack with range 4.
Impassable Walls
WT
Water Tower Impassable Wall
Footprint: 1 panel · Blocks: Movement, Line of Sight, Projectiles · HP: Indestructible
  • Cannot be moved through, attacked through or destroyed by any means.
  • Champions with Elevation Bypass may path over this obstacle if Floor 2 connects above it.
Hazards
SK
Skylight Hazard · Fragile Floor
Footprint: 1 panel · Blocks: Nothing · HP: 100
  • Stepping onto this panel deals 150 true damage to the moving Champion unless they have EVA 25%+ or are airborne.
  • If destroyed (manually or by being stepped on while at low HP), the tile is converted to a Void / Gap for the rest of the battle — the Champion who broke it falls to Floor 1 if one exists below.
  • Indestructible to projectile damage; only melee or fall impact can break a skylight.
Elevation Features
FE
Fire Escape Traversable Elevation
Footprint: 1 panel · Connects: Floor 1 ↔ Floor 2 · HP: Indestructible
  • Champions may spend 2 STA to move between Floor 1 and Floor 2 from this panel without using a Special.
  • Champions with Elevation Bypass ignore the STA cost.
  • Counts as Floor 1 for the purposes of targeting and abilities while the Champion is standing on the panel.
Interactive Elements
PC
Pigeon Coop Interactive · One-Time Use
Footprint: 1 panel · Blocks: Line of Sight · HP: 200
  • A Champion adjacent to this obstacle may spend 1 STA as a free action to open the coop, releasing a flock that grants Stealth (Common) for 1 turn to all allies within 3 panels.
  • One-time use per battle; the coop becomes a Destructible Cover obstacle after being triggered.
  • Destroying the coop without opening it loses the Stealth effect.
Changelog

Balance changes since this Battlefield's release. Newest first.

v1.1.0 May 27, 2026
  • Nerf AC Housing — HP reduced from 800 → 600. Cover value reduced from 50% → 40% DEF.
  • Rework Skylight — Now converts to a Void / Gap when destroyed, rather than a permanent Floor panel. The Champion who breaks it now falls to Floor 1 if one exists below.
  • Buff Pigeon Coop — Added a one-time Stealth-granting interaction. Previously was a plain Destructible Cover.

AC Housing was disproportionately tanky for a 1-panel destructible cover, allowing ranged-comp teams to camp behind it indefinitely on this otherwise mobility-rewarding stage. The skylight rework restores a real risk for melee Champions trying to use the rooftops as a bridge — falling matters again. The pigeon coop change adds a one-time tactical option to a previously plain obstacle, giving stealth-leaning teams a reason to detour toward the corners of the map.
