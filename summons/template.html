Champion Mimic

Prismatic Echo

Booster

Champion Mimic is a prismatic construct conjured by The Summoner — a body without a fighting style until it borrows one. It reads whichever ally is in front of it and adopts pieces of them: a stat here, a stance there, an ULTIMATUM cadence. It doesn't have an identity in the usual sense; it has a knack for wearing someone else's. That's what makes it useful. It's also why, when The Summoner falls, the Mimic loses the pattern it was holding to and starts swinging for whatever's left.

"I am not you. But I might as well be."

Combat Kit

Fixed kit — summons don't swap abilities
Basic Ability
Adaptive Strike Basic
Type: Adaptive · Target: One Enemy · Energy: None · Cooldown: None · Range: 1–5 (adapts to target)
  • Deals 130% damage using the higher of the Champion Mimic's ATK or MAG.
  • Automatically resolves as melee when the target is adjacent, ranged when the target is further away.
Special Abilities
Mimicry Special 1
Type: Support · Target: One Ally · Energy: 30 · Cooldown: 3 turns · Range: 5
  • Copies 50% of the target ally's higher offensive stat (ATK or MAG), applying it to the Champion Mimic's own stats for 2 turns.
  • Also copies one random Tag from the ally's Tags panel for the same duration.
  • Can be cast on the same ally consecutively but the copied Tag re-rolls each cast.
Aegis Duplicate Special 2
Type: Support · Target: One Ally · Energy: 40 · Cooldown: 3 turns · Range: 5
  • Grants the target ally +25% DEF and RES for 2 turns.
  • Grants the target ally "Overhealth" equal to 200% of Champion Mimic's HEAL for 2 turns.
  • Champion Mimic gains the same buffs at 50% strength, letting it double as a partial tank.
Passive Abilities
Adaptive Form Passive 1
  • At the start of each of Champion Mimic's turns, adjusts base stats (ATK/MAG/DEF/RES) to match the average of the current living team, scaled to 90%.
  • While any ally is below 30% HP, Champion Mimic's Special cooldowns are reduced by 1 turn.
  • Cannot be dispelled by enemies.
Echo of the Summoner Passive 2
  • Champion Mimic persists indefinitely as long as The Summoner is alive on the battlefield.
  • If The Summoner is defeated, Champion Mimic gains "Enraged" and +30% ATK and MAG for 3 turns, then dispels back to the summon queue.
  • While The Summoner is alive, Champion Mimic's ULT gauge fills at +15% rate.
ULTIMATUM
Total Mimicry ULTIMATUM
Type: Support · Target: One Ally · Range: 8 · Cost: 100% ULT
  • Champion Mimic fully copies the target ally's active ability slots for 1 turn: Basic, both Specials, both Passives and ULTIMATUM become available on Champion Mimic.
  • Copied abilities use Champion Mimic's own stats (post-Adaptive Form).
  • Cannot copy an ULTIMATUM that requires self-only targeting (e.g., defensive last-stand ULTs); those slots resolve as no-ops.
  • The Mimic cannot copy the same ally twice per battle.
