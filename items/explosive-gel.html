Explosive Gel

Deployable

The Explosive Gel is a gadget and occasional weapon. The user can spray the gel on weak structures and detonate it at will. When triggered, any enemy in the blast radius is knocked to the ground.

Tags
Direct Damage EN Drain STA Drain Area of Effect

Effect

Item Ability
Detonate Deployable
Type: Deployable · Target: One Panel + AoE (3 panels) · Range: 2
  • When used for the first time, places explosive gel on the targeted panel.
  • When used again, detonates the explosive gel and damages any obstacle, ally or enemy within a 3-panel radius around the targeted panel for 500 damage.
  • Deals an additional 800 damage to obstacles.
  • Any allies or enemies damaged by the explosive gel lose 50 EN and 4 STA.
  • Consumed on second usage (when gel is activated).
