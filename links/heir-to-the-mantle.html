Heir to the Mantle

Champions who have inherited a hero's identity from someone who came before — whether by choice, circumstance or tragedy. They carry the weight of a name that isn't quite theirs yet, fighting to live up to it while quietly forging it into something new. Heirs tend to be younger, less polished and more uncertain than the originals, but they bring fresh perspective and a refusal to be defined only by their predecessor.
