Dagger

Tandy Bowen

Healer

A runaway heiress who survived an experiment that killed everyone else it touched, Tandy Bowen fights as Dagger — a wielder of living light that mends as readily as it wounds.

Tandy Bowen grew up with everything money could buy and nothing a child actually needs. Raised in wealth by a mother far more interested in her own life than her daughter's, she learned early that a person can be surrounded by comfort and still be utterly alone — and when she finally ran from it, she ran straight into a city that took the last of what she had within hours. What she found there instead was a boy with nothing at all, who needed her; and for the first time in her life, Tandy mattered to someone. The men who abducted them both, and the drug they were made to survive when no one else did, only forged that into permanence. What's remarkable about her is what she chose to do with a childhood of neglect: rather than harden, she became the warmth she was never given, incapable of walking past a frightened kid who feels unwanted because she knows precisely how that feels. She is the fire in their partnership and the fiercer half of it — the one who pushes forward when Tyrone falters, and the one who steps in front of him when the world recoils from the darkness he carries.

The power she was given answers to that same nature. Tandy conjures daggers of pure living light and hurls them from her hands, blades that sap the strength and will from whoever they strike — yet the very same light is a balm as much as a weapon, capable of burning poison out of the blood, soothing a troubled mind, and lifting the grip of addiction from those the world had already given up on. It is a gift with a price, all the same: the light does not stop gathering inside her, and left to build, it will consume its own bearer from within. Only the void that Cloak carries can safely take the excess. So Tandy survives by giving her light away — which may be the truest thing about her, a girl offered nothing in her whole childhood who became an endless source of the one thing she never received.

"I've got plenty of light to give and you're damn lucky to have it. I hope you know that. The church might be broken, the walls could fall in any minute, but the vows Ty...The vows still stand."

Balance w/ Cloak

Tandy was raised amid every luxury and loved by no one, until she met a boy with nothing at all who needed her — and what the experiment made of them afterward only sealed it. The light that would burn her alive if she ever held it too long has exactly one safe place to go; the hunger that gnaws at Tyrone without end has exactly one thing that can quiet it. But she has never once thought of it as a bargain. The world flinches at the darkness he carries, and Tandy is the one who plants herself in front of him when it does — and she would sooner burn out entirely than leave him to face what lives inside him alone.

Changelog

Balance changes since this Champion's release. Newest first.

v1.1.0 July 2, 2026
  • Rework Healing rebalanced around the "Light" status. Direct healing output is reduced across the board (still substantial), refocusing Dagger as a healing amplifier that scales with the team — and with other healers — rather than out-healing the roster outright.
  • Nerf Light Pierce (Basic): no longer heals allies. Now restores 4% HP to self if the attack lands.
  • Nerf CRIT RATE reduced 75% → 50%. CRIT reduced 125% → 120%. EVA reduced 25% → 20%.
  • Nerf Lightforce Veil: healing reduced 400% → 150% HEAL. Still applies "Light" to all allies in the line.
  • Nerf Dagger Storm: healing reduced 400% → 300% HEAL. Cooldown increased 3 → 4 turns.
  • Rework Precise Dagger: now a guaranteed critical hit. Energy increased 20 → 40, range reduced 15 → 12.
  • Rework Bright Beacon: now evades the next received attack (expires after 1 turn). Cooldown increased 4 → 6 turns.
  • Nerf Master of the Lightforce (Core): ULT gain now triggers only when Dagger's own ability lands. Removed the ULT gain from every "Light"-healed ally, slowing her ULT economy and domain uptime.
  • Rework Corrected ULTIMATUM target descriptions: Light Unleashed now reads "All Allies" (ally damage-reduction domain); Darkness in the Light now reads "All Enemies" (enemy ATK/MAG debuff domain).
At v1.0.0 Dagger was the strongest healer in the roster by a wide margin — a free team-healing basic, heals that critically struck 75% of the time, cheap high-percentage AoE heals, and a core that snowballed ULT off every "Light"-healed ally. This pass keeps her identity as a premier sustain healer but pulls the raw numbers back so healing smooths attrition rather than negating burst, and reworks her toward the "Light" amplifier fantasy — a support whose value grows alongside the rest of the team instead of eclipsing it. Survivability and ULT economy were also tightened to keep the Cloak/Dagger domain combination investment-gated.
